RAMEN

The word "ramen" is a Japanese borrowing of the Mandarin Chinese word lāmiàn, meaning 'pulled noodles. served in a broth; common flavors are soy sauce and miso, with typical toppings including sliced pork (chāshū), nori (dried seaweed), menma (bamboo shoots), and scallions.

POKE SALAD

Poke is diced raw fish served either as an appetizer or a main course and is a popular dish in Hawaii. Traditional forms are aku and heʻe. Heʻe poke is sometimes called tako poke in places where the Hawaiian language is not spoken.
