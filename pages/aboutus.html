About Us

Welcome to HTMT (How To Make This), your ultimate destination for easy and delicious recipes from around the world. We are a website dedicated to helping people create mouthwatering dishes with simplicity and flair. Whether you're a seasoned chef or a beginner in the kitchen, we've got you covered. At HTMT, we believe that cooking should be a joyful and accessible experience for everyone. That's why we curate a wide range of recipes that are not only tasty but also simple to follow. From traditional classics to innovative fusion creations, our collection has something to satisfy every palate. But we offer more than just recipes. We are also proud to present a selection of cookbooks showcasing diverse culinary cultures and traditions. Our carefully curated collection includes books from various countries and regions, allowing you to explore new flavors and techniques from the comfort of your own kitchen. Based in Argentina, HTMT has grown from a local platform to a global community. We are passionate about sharing our love for food and culture with people all over the world. That's why we are excited to announce our plans to expand internationally. Soon, we'll be bringing our wealth of recipes, cookbooks, and culinary expertise to food enthusiasts in every corner of the globe. Stay tuned for updates as we embark on this exciting journey. In the meantime, join us in discovering the joy of cooking and exploring the flavors of different cultures. Let HTMT be your guide to creating delicious meals that will impress your family and friends.
